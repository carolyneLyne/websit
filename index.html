Uganda's Export Logistics Transformation

We exist to drive logistics growth.

Uganda's logistics Foundation driving export growth through stakeholder dialogue, policy reform and the pursuit of logistics infrastructure and systems development.

Uganda has the produce, the ambition and the people.

What it needs is an apex body that connects the right institutions and stakeholders to provide logistics infrastructure and advocacy - supporting every exporter to have a fair shot at global markets.

That is what we are built for.

WHO WE BRING TOGETHER

One Table. Every Player.

Uganda's logistics challenge is too complex for any one sector to solve alone. ELSF is the platform where every stakeholder in the value chain has a seat, a voice, and a role.

🏢 Government Ministries

Trade, Agriculture, Finance, Infrastructure - driving policy and regulatory reform.

🚚 Logistics Operators

Freight forwarders, cargo consolidators, customs clearers and transporters.

🌍 Exporters & MSMEs

Farmers, agro-processors and manufacturers accessing regional and global markets.

🏦 Financial Institutions

Banks and development financiers providing export financing and trade credit.

🤝 Development Partners

Regional and international organisations providing technical and investment support.

❄️ Cold Chain & Warehousing

Facility operators ensuring quality preservation from farm to international market.

⚖️ Regulatory

Standards, certification and compliance agencies enabling export trade readiness.

💡 Research & Innovation

Universities, think tanks and technology players advancing logistics innovation.

Uganda

Export Logistics Support Foundation (ELSF) promotes efficient logistics, trade development and competitiveness for sustainable economic growth in Uganda.
