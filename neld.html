Uganda's premier national forum, where the people who set policy and the people who live with it finally sit at the same table. Organised and convened by ELSF.

About NELD

For years, Ugandan exporters carried the weight of a logistics system that wasn't built around them. Unpredictable transport costs. Financing that wasn't available. Cold-chain infrastructure that existed on paper but not in practice. Trade agreements that opened doors but gave businesses no help walking through them.

The frustration was - and remains - real. But the conversations about fixing it were happening in separate rooms.

NELD was created to change that: to give Uganda's logistics challenges one national home where all the institutions capable of driving change could hear the people experiencing those challenges firsthand, and be held accountable to responding.

Who Sat at the NELD Table

🏢 Government

MoWT, MoFPED, MTIC, Free Zones Authority, trade authorities and standards institutions.

🌍 Exporters & MSMEs

Businesses at every scale - from smallholder farmers and food processors to large commodity exporters and manufacturers.

🚚 Logistics Sector

Freight forwarders, cargo clearers, transport operators, consolidators, warehouse managers and cold-chain providers.

🏦 Financial Institutions

Institutions explaining export financing structures, risk frameworks and opportunities for MSMEs.

🤝 Development Partners

USAID, UNDP and other organisations bringing technical insight, funding and regional perspective.
