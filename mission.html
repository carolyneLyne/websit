OUR FOCUS

Comprehensive Logistics Support Across Every Level

ELSF operates across the full logistics value chain - from policy to farm gate, and warehouse floor to regional trade corridor.

🏢❄️ Warehousing & Cold Chain

Facilitating access to sufficient cold-chain infrastructure and warehousing so that Uganda's perishable exports can reach premium, competitive markets.

🚚 Logistics System Development

Reducing inefficiencies along export corridors by fortifying the end-to-end logistics architecture - from freight clearance and cargo consolidation to last-mile delivery.

🌍 Free Zones & Special Economic Areas

Facilitating the growth of free zones as key hubs for export processing, opening up competitive infrastructure and regulatory frameworks for manufacturers.

💰 Export Cost Optimisation

Locating and eliminating the cost layers of transportation, handling and compliance, and linking exporters to available subsidies and incentives that reduce Uganda's export margins.

🤝 Public-Private Coordination

Serving as the structured interface between government institutions and private-sector logistics players, ensuring reforms are designed with operational realities in mind.

WHAT DRIVES US

Our core values.

These aren't words on a wall. They are the principles we hold ourselves to in every dialogue we organise, every program we run and every partnership we build.

Collaboration

We believe the best answers to Uganda's logistics challenges come when every voice in the chain is heard. We build the tables where those conversations happen - and we hold space for the smallest operator with the same respect we give the largest ministry.

Innovation

Uganda's logistics challenges are complex and deeply rooted. We meet them with practical, forward-looking solutions - not theoretical frameworks. Where technology helps, we deploy it. Where new models are needed, we design them.

Sustainability

We plan for a logistics system that works today and doesn't damage tomorrow - for the environment, for communities, and for the next generation of Ugandan exporters who will inherit the systems we build now.

Inclusivity

The smallest exporter in the most remote district deserves the same quality of logistics support as the largest company in Kampala.

We design every program, every policy position and every membership benefit around that belief.

Transparency

We keep our stakeholders informed on what we are doing, what we are learning, and what still needs work.

Trust is built through honest communication, not polished messaging.

Integrity

In a sector where relationships and contracts touch millions of shillings worth of goods every day, we hold ourselves to the highest standards of accountability and ethical conduct. Always.
