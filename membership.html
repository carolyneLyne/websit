WHY JOIN

Uganda's logistics agenda is being shaped right now.

Be part of it.

The conversations happening at NELD, the programs being built through PPELD, and the policy positions ELSF is advocating - all of these will shape Uganda's trade environment for years to come. ELSF membership puts your organisation inside that process, not outside looking in.

Membership Value

A Voice in National Policy

ELSF members participate in NELD and directly influence the regulatory reforms, infrastructure investments and trade-facilitation priorities that affect your business.

A Sector Network You Can Use

Membership connects you to a cross-sector network of government bodies, development partners, financial institutions and fellow logistics operators.

Training & Capacity Programs

Access workshops, technical training and knowledge resources designed specifically for Uganda's logistics realities.

Financing Pathways

ELSF actively advocates for and connects members to tailored financing solutions designed around how logistics businesses actually work.

Research & Market Intelligence

Members receive research, sector analysis, policy briefings and market intelligence to make better business decisions.

Direct Program Participation

Members get priority access to PPELD programs - from warehousing development to digital integration and food-trade logistics.
